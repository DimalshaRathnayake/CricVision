Research Overview

Everything cricket video analysis needs

The system supports player-wise filtering, event-wise navigation, timestamp-based player-event association, batsman and bowler change tracking, and fast search of important cricket moments.

OCR

Scoreboard Intelligence

Extracts batsmen, bowler names, scores, overs, and contextual match details from visible scoreboard regions.

CV

Event Detection

Uses deep visual models to detect umpire presence and classify important cricket signals such as FOUR, SIX, and WICKET.

NLP

Player Identification

Combines OCR cleaning, NER, and roster matching to identify players and reduce noisy text detections.

AI

Semantic Retrieval

Indexes video events with embeddings and vector search so users can find moments through natural language queries.
